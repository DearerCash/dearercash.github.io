Don't overmix

A few lumps in the batter are fine — overmixing develops gluten and makes pancakes tough.

🌡️

Rest the batter

Let your batter sit for 5–10 minutes before cooking so the bubbles relax and you get a fluffier result.

🔥

Medium heat wins

Too hot and you'll burn the outside before the centre cooks. Medium-low is your friend.

🧈

Butter the pan right

Wipe excess butter with a paper towel — too much causes uneven browning and a greasy bottom.

⏱️

Flip only once

Wait until bubbles form on the surface and edges look set before flipping. One flip only!

🥛

Room temp ingredients

Cold eggs and milk cause uneven mixing. Let them come to room temp before starting.
